## üì≤ FILMING TIPS FOR PHONE

### Lighting:
- Sit facing a window (natural light)
- OR use a $20 ring light from Amazon
- OR film outside in shade (not direct sun)

### Framing:
- Hold phone vertically (9:16 ratio)
- Put your face in top 2/3 of frame
- Leave room for text overlays at top

### Audio:
- Film in a quiet room
- OR use iPhone earbuds as mic (better than phone mic)
- OR add trending music to cover bad audio

### Editing:
- Use **CapCut** (free, easy, has templates)
- Use **TikTok editor** (built-in, fast)
- Keep it **under 20 seconds** for max retention
