## The 7 Core Rules

### 1. Layer Multiple Hooks
Stack unlimited hooks. Don't rely on one.
- 7 types: Visual, text, environment, comment, spoken, ASMR, story
- Mini hooks every 15-20s: "But here's where it gets crazy..."

Advanced: [Emotional Stacking](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#a-emotional-word-stacking), [Pattern Language](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#b-pattern-language-framework), [Binary Tension](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#c-binary-choice-tension)

### 2. Never Look Like an Ad
The second you look like an ad, you're done.
- ❌ "Do you have [problem]? Introducing..."
- ✓ "So yesterday..." (conversational, organic)

UGC Rule: User is the hero, not the product.

Advanced: [Protective Framing](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#protective-framing-for-ctas), [UGC Structure](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#ugc-script-structure)

### 3. Visual Change Every 2-3 Seconds
Change something every 2-3s: zoom, text, B-roll, sound, transition.

Advanced: [Visual Rhythm](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#visual-rhythm--pacing), [Slideshow Specs](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#slideshow-specific-guidelines)

### 4. Structure as Story, Not Ad
5-Act Arc: Setup → Escalation → Discovery → Transformation → Resolution
- NOT: "Here are 5 features..."
- IS: "I made the worst trading mistake..."

Advanced: [Story Structure](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#story-structure-details)

### 5. Fix Drop-Off Points
Find where viewers leave. Inject retention hooks at exact moments.

Advanced: [Drop-Off Analysis](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#drop-off-analysis-framework)

### 6. Hyper-Specific Targeting
Make ONE person say "Finally, this!"
- Bad: "Investors"
- Good: "Retail investors who've lost money following social media tips and are skeptical of AI but desperate for better analysis"

Advanced: [Targeting Framework](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#hyper-specific-targeting)

### 7. Every Sentence Earns the Next
Zero filler. Each sentence creates curiosity/value to justify staying.

Advanced: [Sentence-Level Retention](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#sentence-level-retention)

## 6 Viral Formulas

Full examples & breakdowns: [VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md](./research/insights/VIRAL_RULES_ADVANCED.md#viral-content-formulas)

1. Comparison/Contrast: "X does this → Y does THIS" (ChatGPT 3hrs vs Bloom 12s)
2. Failure Story: "I lost $8,400 → Here's what I learned"
3. Educational Breakdown: "3 things ChatGPT can't do for investing"
4. Pattern Interrupt: "WAIT STOP! Before you buy..."
5. Time/Effort Comparison: "3 hours vs 12 seconds for same result"
6. Dangerous Confidence: "ChatGPT doesn't know it's wrong"

---

## Duration Guidelines

- 15-30s: Pattern interrupts, quick reveals
- 30-45s: Time comparisons, transformations
- 45-60s: Full narratives, experiments
- 5-10+ min: Personalized segments

Hook = 0-3s. No preamble.

---

## Authenticity Rules

ALWAYS:
- Real screenshots with timestamps
- Actual stock prices and dates
- Source citations
- Mark simulated vs real experiments

NEVER:
- Fabricate numbers
- Exaggerate losses/gains
- Use fake conversations
- Claim results without proof
