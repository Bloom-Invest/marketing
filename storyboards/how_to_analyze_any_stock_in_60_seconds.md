EDUCATIONAL FRAMEWORK:
**The 5-Step Stock Analysis Framework:**

**Step 1: Valuation**
- Check P/E ratio vs industry average
- Quick metric: P/E, P/S, or EV/EBITDA
- Goal: Determine if stock is cheap, fair, or expensive

**Step 2: Growth**
- Check revenue and earnings growth
- Compare to industry and historical averages
- Goal: Determine if business is expanding

**Step 3: Risk**
- Check debt-to-equity ratio
- Look at cash flow and liquidity
- Goal: Assess financial stability

**Step 4: Competitive Moat**
- Check market share
- Assess pricing power and brand strength
- Goal: Determine competitive advantage

**Step 5: Verdict**
- Synthesize all 4 data points
- Make buy/hold/sell decision
- Goal: Actionable conclusion
