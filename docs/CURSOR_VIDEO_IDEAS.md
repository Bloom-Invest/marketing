## ğŸ¬ PRODUCTION TIPS

### Visual Style:
- **Fast cuts** - Change scene every 2-3 seconds
- **Text overlays** - Key numbers and takeaways
- **Green screen** - You + Bloom AI interface
- **Charts/graphs** - Animated, bold colors
- **Progress bars** - Show AI "thinking"
- **Split screens** - Comparisons

### Audio:
- **Fast-paced voiceover** - Energetic, confident
- **Sound effects** - Cash register, alerts, whooshes
- **Background music** - Subtle, doesn't compete with VO

### Hook Formula:
1. **Pattern interrupt** (0-1 sec): "NO WAY" / "WATCH THIS" / "YOU WON'T BELIEVE"
2. **Value promise** (1-3 sec): What they'll learn/gain
3. **Curiosity gap** (3-5 sec): Hint at the payoff

### Call-to-Action:
- "Link in bio for full analysis"
- "Comment 'BLOOM' for free stock picks"
- "Follow for daily AI insights"
- "Tag someone who needs to see this"
