Visual Style:

• Stock charts and financial data animated
• Real screenshots of disclosures when possible
• Split screens for before/after comparisons

Audio:
• Direct, conversational voiceover
• Occasional dramatic pauses
• Background music: Minimal, suspenseful
• Sound effects on key reveals

Pacing:
• Fast cuts (2-3 seconds per frame)
• Text overlays for emphasis
• Numbers always visible and large
• CTAs clear and repeated

Engagement Tactics:
• Ask questions in captions
• Use controversial statements
• Tag politicians (carefully)
• Encourage comments with specific questions
• Promise follow-ups for engagement

Legal Considerations:
• Use public record disclosures only
• Avoid direct accusations
• Present data, let viewers conclude
• Include disclaimer about not being financial advice
• Clearly label opinions vs facts
