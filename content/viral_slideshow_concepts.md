# Viral Slideshow Concepts for Bloom AI
## 30-Day Content Calendar for Value Investors

**Target Audience**: Value investors seeking AI-powered research to make winning trades

**Platform Strategy**: TikTok/Instagram Reels slideshow format (7-15 seconds per slide)

---

## Key Viral Patterns Identified

### Hook Types That Drive 500K+ Engagement:
1. **Contrarian Takes** - "Everyone believes X, but here's what the rich actually do..."
2. **Pattern Interrupts** - "No, have you seen what happens to..."
3. **Direct Value Promise** - "If you wanna get rich in your 20s..."
4. **Us vs. Them** - "Poor people are terrified, rich people are stoked..."
5. **Simplification** - "Rich people set up their kids for success and it's not even complicated..."
6. **Urgency/FOMO** - "This just happened right before our very eyes..."

### Engagement Drivers:
- **First 3 seconds**: Bold, controversial, or curiosity-driven statement
- **Visual contrast**: Before/after, rich vs poor, expected vs reality
- **Relatable pain points**: Missed opportunities, bad decisions, confusion
- **Actionable insights**: Specific steps, not vague advice
- **Status/identity**: "Here's what winners do that losers don't..."

## Production Notes

### Visual Style:
- **Text**: Bold, large, high-contrast (white text on dark bg or vice versa)
- **Transitions**: Fast cuts (0.5-1 second per slide)
- **Charts/Data**: Annotated with arrows, circles, highlights
- **Branding**: Bloom AI logo watermark, consistent color scheme (suggest: deep blue + neon green)

### Audio Strategy:
- **Trending sounds**: Use TikTok trending audio library
- **Voiceover**: Optional, but adds authority (use AI voice for consistency)
- **Text-to-speech**: TikTok native TTS for accessibility + algorithm boost

### CTAs:
- Every video ends with: "Link in bio" or "Search Bloom AI"
- Pin comment with specific call-to-action
- Occasional "stitch this" or "duet if you agree" for virality

### Hashtag Strategy:
- Core: #BloomAI #StockResearch #Investing
- Viral: #FinanceTikTok #StockMarket #ValueInvesting #MoneyTok
- Trending: Check daily, add 1-2 trending general hashtags

### Posting Schedule:
- Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM EST (when finance people scroll)
- Consistency: Same time daily for algorithm
- Platform: TikTok primary, auto-crosspost to Instagram Reels

## Success Metrics to Track

1. **Hook retention**: % who watch past 3 seconds
2. **Completion rate**: % who watch full video
3. **Engagement rate**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Views
4. **Click-through**: Bio link clicks per view
5. **Shareability**: Shares per view (aim for 2%+)

Target benchmarks (based on viral finance content):
- 500K+ views = viral success
- 50K+ likes = strong engagement
- 5%+ engagement rate = algorithm loves it
- 1%+ share rate = organic amplification

---

## A/B Testing Ideas

Test weekly:
- **Hook variations**: Question vs Statement vs Stat
- **CTA placement**: End card vs mid-video vs pinned comment
- **Video length**: 7 sec vs 15 sec vs 30 sec
- **Text vs Voice**: Silent with text vs voiceover
- **Controversy level**: Safe takes vs hot takes
