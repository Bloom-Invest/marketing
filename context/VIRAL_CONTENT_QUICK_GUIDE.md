## ✓ DO THESE (High Impact)

### 1. Use Urgency Language (80% of top videos)
**Power Words:** now, today, right now, before, immediately, don't wait

**Example:** "Right now, Wall Street just dropped $13 billion..."

### 2. Create Relatable Scenarios (73% of top videos)
**Phrases:** "If you...", "You know when...", "We all...", "Most people..."

**Example:** "If you blew all your money in your twenties on shoes and food..."

### 3. Include Clear Call-to-Action (67% of top videos)
**Options:** Link in bio, follow for more, comment below, share this, tag someone

**Example:** "Link in bio to learn this for free"

### 4. Tell Personal Stories (63% of top videos)
**Phrases:** "I was...", "When I...", "I did...", "Years ago I..."

**Example:** "I went from $40k debt to millionaire at 30..."

### 5. Use Bold Statements, Not Questions (85% of top videos)
**Instead of:** "Have you ever wondered why..."
**Use:** "Everyone believes X, but the truth is..."

---

## ✗ AVOID THESE (Low Performance)

1. **Opening with questions** - Bottom performers use questions 59% more often
2. **60-75 second videos** - The "dead zone" for engagement
3. **Academic tone** - Use conversational language instead
4. **Generic advice** - Be specific with numbers and examples
5. **No urgency** - Creates passive viewing instead of action

## The Viral Video Formula

### Hook (0-3 seconds) - Choose ONE:
- **Relatable Callout:** "If you're someone who [problem]..."
- **Bold Statement:** "Everyone believes [myth], but..."
- **Pattern Interrupt:** "Stop! Don't [action]..."
- **Dramatic Opening:** "This just happened..."

### Value Proposition (3-7 seconds):
- State what viewer will learn
- Use specific numbers
- Create curiosity gap

### Content (7-45 seconds) - Use 4-5 techniques:
1. Personal story
2. Relatable scenario
3. Contrarian view
4. Urgency language
5. Statistics/examples

### CTA (Last 3-5 seconds):
- Link in bio
- Follow for more
- Comment/share

---

## Optimal Video Length

| Duration | Engagement | When to Use |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| **5-10s** | 6-7.6% | Quick tips, hooks, viral moments |
| **25s** | 6.7% | Sweet spot for most content |
| **55s** | 6.8% | Educational with story |
| **100-120s** | 8.5%+ | Deep dives (HIGH RISK/REWARD) |
| **60-75s** | 3.6-4.1% | ✗ AVOID - Dead zone |

**Strategy:** Go short (5-25s) OR long (100s+). Avoid the middle.

## Quick Templates

### Template 1: Contrarian Hook
```
"Everyone believes [common belief], but the truth is [contrarian view]
because [reason]. I did [action] and [result]. Link in bio."
```

### Template 2: Relatable Callout
```
"If you [relatable struggle], this is for you. Here's what [authority]
won't tell you... Instead of [common approach], do [better approach].
Follow for more."
```

### Template 3: Pattern Interrupt
```
"Stop! Don't [common action]... I made this mistake and [bad outcome].
Here's what you should do instead... Comment if you've done this."
```

### Template 4: Bold Statement
```
"[Provocative statement]. If you [reaction], it means [psychology].
Instead, [better mindset]. Share this with someone who needs it."
```

### Template 5: News Hook
```
"[Current event] just happened. Here's what this means for your [money].
If you have $[amount], here's what to do... Follow for daily updates."
```
