## ‚úÖ PRODUCTION GUIDELINES FOR MAXIMUM ENGAGEMENT

### Video Structure
- **First 3 seconds**: Bold claim, shocking stat, or pattern interrupt
- **Format**: Text overlays + fast cuts (every 2-3 seconds)
- **Length**: 15-45 seconds optimal (can extend to 60s for detailed breakdowns)
- **Pacing**: Quick, energetic, no dead air

### Content Elements
- **Visual hierarchy**: Large text overlays for key points
- **Proof**: Screenshots, charts, real numbers (never generic)
- **Transitions**: Snap cuts, no fades
- **Music**: Trending audio or dramatic background music

### Call-to-Action
- "Follow for daily trade alerts"
- "Link in bio for free research"
- "Comment 'STOCKS' for the full list"
- "Save this before it gets taken down"

### Engagement Triggers
- **Controversy**: Political trades, "insider" knowledge, class warfare themes
- **Specificity**: Exact dollar amounts, specific stocks, named politicians
- **Urgency**: "Before it's too late", "while everyone else panics"
- **Exclusivity**: "No one's talking about", "hidden pattern", "under the radar"

### Visual Style
- Screen recordings of actual Bloom AI interface
- Charts and data visualizations
- News headlines for context
- Stock price graphs with annotations
- Before/after comparisons
